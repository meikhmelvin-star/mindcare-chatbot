Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using MindCare, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use MindCare, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address and password when you register
  • Chat messages: The conversations you have with MindCare
  • Assessment results: Your answers and scores from mental health assessments
  • Usage data: How you use the platform to help us improve it

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and personalise your MindCare experience
  • Store your chat history so you can refer back to it
  • Track your assessment results over time
  • Improve our chatbot responses and features
  • Send you important account notifications

3. Information Sharing

We take your privacy very seriously. We never sell, trade or share your personal information with third parties, except in these limited cases:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the safety of our users or the public
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Your data is stored in a secure database and protected against unauthorised access. We use industry-standard security practices to keep your information safe. However, no system is 100% secure, so we encourage you to use a strong password and keep it confidential.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
