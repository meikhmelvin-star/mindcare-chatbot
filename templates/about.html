Our Story

About MindCare

We built MindCare because we believe everyone deserves access to mental health support — anytime, anywhere, for free.

Our Mission

Making Mental Health Support Accessible to All

Mental health challenges affect millions of people worldwide, yet many never get the help they need due to cost, stigma, or lack of access. MindCare was built to change that.

Our platform provides a safe, private space where anyone can talk about how they're feeling, take mental health assessments, and get pointed toward professional help when needed.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Everything we do is guided by these core principles

Privacy First

Your conversations are completely private. We never share your data with anyone, ever.

Compassion

We approach every interaction with empathy, care and zero judgment.

Accessibility

Free, available 24/7 and designed to work on any device — for everyone.

Safety

We take your wellbeing seriously and always point you to professional help when needed.
