FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MindCare free to use?

Yes! MindCare is completely free. You can register, chat, and take assessments at no cost.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your conversations are stored securely and never shared with any third party.

Is MindCare a replacement for professional therapy?

No. MindCare provides support and guidance but is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are in crisis, please contact a professional.
